## Company

SkyBot is an innovative startup revolutionizing aviation training with an AI-powered simulator designed to enhance communication skills. By simulating real-world scenarios, we empower student pilots and professionals to excel in air traffic control interactions with confidence and precision.

At SkyBot, we believe in making aviation training accessible, flexible, and highly effective through cutting-edge technology tailored for the challenges of modern aviation.
